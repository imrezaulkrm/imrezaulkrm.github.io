About Me

Hey, I’m Md Rezaul Karim — a DevOps enthusiast from Bangladesh who’s passionate about building automated, scalable, and resilient systems.

I’m on a journey to become an advanced-level engineer by exploring the world of infrastructure and automation. I love solving real-world challenges and constantly push myself to grow through hands-on experience and continuous learning.

My interests lie in understanding how technology can improve both system reliability and developer experience. I also enjoy sharing knowledge, reflecting on what I learn, and building meaningful projects that make an impact.

I’m always eager to connect with like-minded individuals, so feel free to reach out if you share my passion for DevOps, automation, or just want to chat about tech!

Outside the world of tech, I find peace in nature, enjoy photography, and unwind with a good cup of coffee.

“Engineering is simplifying complexity, automating the mundane, and building trust.”
